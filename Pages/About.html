About Us

Sherpa's Bookstore has been providing service since 1976. Our services have been changed a lot but our legacy is the same, to provide books to read.
We are committed to providing quality books, magazines, novels, and more at
affordable prices. Our focus is to provide books and services of all types while making knowledge and education expand. knowledge is something that we can never get enough of, the more people read, more their intelligence grows and so does our business! We are located in 152 different locations througout the world. We believe reading can change the world!
